Information We Collect

We gather non-identifying analytics data to measure readership trends, page performance, and navigation patterns. Any optional details shared through our contact form remain confidential and are used solely to respond to inquiries.

Cookies and Tracking

Cursed Chronicles employs lightweight cookies to remember reader preferences, such as spoiler warnings or theme selections. Third-party analytics scripts, when active, observe aggregate behaviors without storing personal identifiers.

Data Usage and Retention

Submitted information is stored only as long as necessary to resolve questions or maintain newsletter subscriptions. We never sell, lease, or otherwise share personal data with unaffiliated entities. Access to stored messages is restricted to authorized editorial members.

Your Rights

Readers may request deletion or modification of personal data by contacting us via the form on the Contact page. We honor reasonable requests within 30 days and document the completion of each request for accountability.
